About

Strategy & people.

"I have always been drawn to two things: strategy and people."

Throughout my career I've built partnerships from scratch, led teams, and co-founded initiatives that make a real dent in the world.

Currently I lead the Latvian Private Equity and Venture Capital Association (LVCA), championing the startup and investment ecosystem in Latvia and the wider CEE region. I am also an active angel investor through BADideas.fund, backing post-MVP startups with €50–150K tickets.

I'm a co-founder of the Global Shapers Riga Hub — a World Economic Forum initiative — and I mentor students navigating career choices and personal growth.

Outside of work you'll find me at a theater or concert, or tucked into a corner with a coffee and a psychology book.
